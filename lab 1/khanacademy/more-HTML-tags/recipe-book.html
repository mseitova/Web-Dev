Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 4
Pecorino Romano 100g
Guanciale 150g
Black Pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, fry the guanciale in a skillet until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs and Pecorino Romano together in a bowl.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and immediately mix it with the guanciale in the skillet.
  5. Remove from heat, then stir in the egg and cheese mixture. Add black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese if desired.

Sushi

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Sushi Rice 2 cups
Rice Vinegar 1/3 cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Seaweed (Nori) 4 sheets
Raw Fish (Salmon, Tuna, etc.) 200g
Vegetables (Cucumber, Avocado, etc.) 1 cup, sliced

Steps:

  1. Cook the sushi rice according to the package instructions and let it cool slightly.
  2. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then stir it into the cooked rice.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat, shiny side down.
  4. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  5. Add slices of fish and vegetables in a line near the bottom edge.
  6. Roll the nori tightly using the bamboo mat and seal the edge with a little water.
  7. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
